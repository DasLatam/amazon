Descripción

STOP BAD BREATH BEFORE IT STARTS

Brushing your teeth isn't enough. A large share of bad breath odor comes from bacteria
and food debris trapped in the coating on your tongue — a spot your toothbrush barely
touches. The Zriceros Tongue Scraper is designed to remove that buildup quickly and
effectively, so you start every day with a genuinely clean, fresh mouth.

WHY STAINLESS STEEL

Unlike plastic tongue scrapers that wear out, crack, or hold onto bacteria in tiny
grooves, our 100% stainless steel design stays smooth and hygienic wash after wash.
It won't rust, won't bend out of shape, and won't degrade with hot water or
dishwasher cycles — making it a long-term upgrade to your oral care routine instead
of a disposable one.

HOW TO USE

1. Stick out your tongue and place the scraper as far back as comfortable.
2. Gently pull the scraper forward toward the tip of your tongue.
3. Rinse the scraper and repeat 3-5 times, moving from back to front.
4. Rinse your mouth and the scraper with water when finished.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

3 stainless steel tongue scrapers — enough to keep one at home, one for travel,
and one as a spare or to share with a family member.

CARE INSTRUCTIONS

Hand wash with mild soap or place on the top rack of your dishwasher. Allow to
air dry. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could scratch the surface.
